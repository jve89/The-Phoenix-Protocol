Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: August 16, 2025

1. What is The Phoenix Protocol?

It’s a daily email program designed to help you recover after heartbreak. Each day you’ll receive a supportive story, reflection, or exercise tailored to your stage.

2. How long does the program last?

You can choose the duration that fits your needs: 7 days, 30 days, or 90 days. Each option is a complete program — just pick the path that works best for you.

3. Can I unsubscribe at any time?

Yes. Each email includes an unsubscribe link. Once unsubscribed, you won’t receive further messages unless you re-subscribe.

4. Is this a subscription?

No. It’s a one-time payment product. You pay once for the full program length you select (7, 30, or 90 days).

5. Can I get a refund?

Because content is delivered digitally and immediately, sales are final. If you encounter an error, contact support@thephoenixprotocol.app.

6. Does this replace therapy?

No. The Phoenix Protocol offers encouragement and structure, but it is not a medical or psychological service. If you’re struggling severely, please reach out to a qualified professional.

7. I can’t find the emails — what should I do?

Sometimes email providers mistakenly flag our content as spam, junk, or place it in the “Promotions” folder. Please check those folders, and add support@thephoenixprotocol.app to your contacts or mark the emails as “Not spam” to ensure smooth delivery.
