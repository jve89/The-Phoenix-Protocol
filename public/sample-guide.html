Day 1: Clarity & Grounding

Today’s focus is stabilising your emotional environment. You will learn how to set gentle boundaries with yourself and your ex, understanding why emotional distance is crucial for your healing process.

Practical Step: A 5-minute grounding exercise you can do upon waking to reduce anxiety and increase clarity.

Day 2: Building Morning Stability

Learn to create a morning routine that helps you start your day with intention instead of being overwhelmed by thoughts of your ex. This day guides you through a 10-minute structured morning practice.

Practical Step: Journal your intentions for the day and one self-care action you will commit to completing today.

Day 3: Reconnecting with Purpose

Today’s guide helps you reconnect with your sense of purpose, even in the midst of emotional turbulence. You will explore what matters to you and how to begin rebuilding your personal identity post-breakup.

Practical Step: A 15-minute reflection exercise on your core values and what you want your next chapter to look like.
